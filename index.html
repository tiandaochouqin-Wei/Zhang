Introduction

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Karolinska Institutet. My research focuses on medical image processing, deep learning algorithms, robot vision, and multimodal artificial intelligence for healthcare applications.

I received my PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology and completed joint doctoral training at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My recent work mainly involves low-dose CT denoising, brain tumor segmentation, pulmonary nodule detection, PET/CT image fusion, and medical image quality assessment.

I am broadly interested in building practical and trustworthy AI methods for biomedical imaging and clinical decision support, and I welcome academic discussion and research collaboration in related areas.
